# Compliance Information

This practice is committed to adhering to the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) regulations and the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). 

## HPCSA Compliance

We ensure that all healthcare professionals are registered with the HPCSA and comply with the ethical guidelines set forth by the council. Our practice prioritizes patient safety and confidentiality in all interactions.

## POPIA Compliance

In accordance with POPIA, we are dedicated to protecting your personal information. We collect only the necessary data required for providing healthcare services and ensure that it is stored securely. You have the right to access your personal information and request corrections if needed.

For more information on our compliance policies, please contact us directly.
