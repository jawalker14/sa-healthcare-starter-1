# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and process personal information in line with POPIA. This page outlines what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

## What we collect

## How we use your data
- To provide healthcare services and respond to your queries
- To manage bookings and reminders

## Your rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at the email listed on this site.

This policy may be updated from time to time.
