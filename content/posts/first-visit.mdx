# Your First Visit: What to Expect

Visiting us for the first time? Here's how to prepare and what will happen during your appointment.

## Before your appointment
- Arrive 10 minutes early for forms
- Bring any relevant medical reports

## During your appointment
- Assessment and history
- Examination and initial treatment
- Personalised plan and next steps

## After your appointment
We'll send you home care tips and, if needed, book your follow-up.
